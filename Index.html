We Design, Build & Manafacture Your Dream Case!

Mark Wood Shell (MWS) is an online phone case business founded by two small businesses in Kent. The Business Mark Special Woods (MSW) supply hardwoods (locally grown & imported.) to Mark Joinery (MJ) then use sophisticated CNC routers and laser cutters to manufacture our amazing cases. Our aim is to provide cool designs with customisable features for any current smartphone from Apple, Samsung, Sony, LG, HTC, Motorola, Nexus with next day delivery.
